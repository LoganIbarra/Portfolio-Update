About

I want to use my UX research skills and personal experiences to create meaningful, inclusive, and accessible products.

As a non-binary person and user advocate, I champion inclusion for all. After seven years of working for a global e-commerce company, I am well-versed in uncovering and communicating user and client needs and providing actionable insights. To positively impact the landscape, I enrolled in the CareerFoundry UX Design program, where I discovered user experience research, which combines my interests in analytical, data-oriented thinking from programming with observational and conversational skills from hospitality.
